My Favorite Movies and TV Shows

Inception

Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi

Description: A skilled thief, who commits corporate espionage by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets, is given a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform an inception.

Stars: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page

The Dark Knight

Genre: Action, Crime, Drama

Description: Batman faces off against the Joker, a criminal mastermind who seeks to create chaos in Gotham City, pushing Batman to his limits and testing his morality.

Stars: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart

Breaking Bad

Genre: Crime, Drama, Thriller

Description: A high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer partners with a former student to build a drug empire. This series explores morality, family, and the consequences of crime.

Stars: Bryan Cranston, Aaron Paul, Anna Gunn

Stranger Things

Genre: Drama, Fantasy, Horror

Description: A group of young friends discover strange events in their small town, including the disappearance of a friend, and uncover a hidden government conspiracy involving supernatural creatures.

Stars: Winona Ryder, Millie Bobby Brown, David Harbour
